1. Find f'(14) if f(x) = sqrt(2x - 3).

2. Find the intervals of monotonicity of the function f(x) = 1 / (x^2 + x).

3. Find the general equation of the tangent to the circle
   (x - 3)^2 + (y - 8)^2 = 20
   at the point (1, 4).

4. The lines that pass through the point (-6, 4) and are tangent to the ellipse
   x^2 / 36 + y^2 / 16 = 1
   are:
   A) x = -6 and y = 4
   B) x = 6 and y = 4
   C) x = -6 and y = -4
   D) x = 6 and y = -4

5. The line 3x + 8y - 50 = 0 is tangent to the ellipse
   x^2 / a^2 + y^2 / 25 = 1.
   Find a^2.
   A) 1
   B) 25
   C) 50
   D) 100

6. The line x - y + 10 = 0 is tangent to the ellipse
   x^2 / 60 + y^2 / 40 = 1
   at point M and intersects the x-axis at point N.
   Find the length MN.
   A) sqrt(2)
   B) 2sqrt(2)
   C) 3sqrt(2)
   D) 4sqrt(2)

7. The point P(50, 7a) is a point of tangency of the line
   Ax - 7y - 4 = 0
   to the hyperbola
   x^2 / a^2 - y^2 / 16 = 1,
   if:
   A) a^2 = 50
   B) a^2 = 16
   C) a^2 = 7
   D) a^2 = 4

8. The line Ax + 3y - 2 = 0 is tangent to the hyperbola
   x^2 / 10 - y^2 / 4 = 1.
   Find A.
   A) 1
   B) 2
   C) 3
   D) 4

9. The line y = 5x + b is tangent to the parabola
   y = x^2 - 5x + 6.
   Find b.
   A) -19
   B) -3
   C) 3
   D) 19

10. Find the intervals of monotonicity, the x-coordinates of the local extrema,
    and the inflection points of the function
    f(x) = x^5 / 5 - x^4 / 2 - 2x^3 + 9x^2 - 27x - 27,
    and draw its graph.

11. Construct the graph of the function
    f(x) = 2x^3 - 5x^2 + 4x - 1.
